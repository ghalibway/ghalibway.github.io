Aakhir mohabbat kya hai..?

Kabhi dard hai, Kabhi dawa hai,
koi bataye aakhir mohabbat kya hai..?

Kahin zakhm hai, kahin marham,
Koi bataye aakhir mohabbat kya hai..?

Kisiko jeeteji maar deti hai, kisi ki zindagi gulzaar kar deti hai,
Koi bataye aakhir mohabbat kya hai..?

El la hasil jawab hai ye ek mukhtasar sawal hai mohabbat,
Main jawab talab karu'n, wo phir sawal kare...
Koi bataye aakhir mohabbat kya hai..?

-Faisal Khatri.
