Let me introduce myself, I am Mohammad Faisal Khatri from Mumbai, the founder and owner of Facebook Page - "Shayri's - The Ghalib Way".

A writer, poet, photographer and a passionate technology lover. I am 

I never imagined to have a community of 89500+ people on facebook. Alhamdolillah, Its by the grace of ALLAH  and your love that keeps me motivated for writing new shayris. 

I was fond of poetry since my childhood, especially of Ghalib sahab’s words, it inspired me a lot to write, and I never imagined to have a community on Social Media, but the hobby gave me an idea to start up a page on Facebook, and I began writing. 

I like writing shayris in classical manner with mixed emotions, in short, I must say, the words should describe the feelings of the heart in two lines.

My main aim is to revive the magic of Mirza Asadullah Khan Ghalib which he created through his Ashaar's in Yester Years.

#faisalkhatri #ghalibway are the hashtags which is used by me on social media sites. These can be found on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.

The following couplet by Ghalib is my favourite.

Wagarna Shehar mein Ghalib ki aabroo kya hai...!!"
