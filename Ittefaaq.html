Ittefaaq

Main chalu jis raah pe, wahin tujhse mulakaat ho,
sochta hun aisa bhi kabhi ittefaaq ho..

Tasawuur karu tera, aur tu saamne aa jaye,
Kya maza ho ke tera zikr karu aur tujhse baat ho..

Isay hi to ishq kehte hain,
Ke main labo’n se kuch na kahu, bayaan nigaho’n se jazbaat ho..

Wo chalkar khud hi aayein izhar e mohabbat karne mujhse,
sochta hun aisa bhi kabhi ittefaaq ho..

-Faisal Khatri.
