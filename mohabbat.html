Mohabbat

Dillagi ka mausam tha, aashiqui ka tha zamaana,
Baharon ka samaa tha, aur uska muskuraana..

Mera dekna tha uski taraf pyar se,
Aur uska sharma k nazrein jhukaana,

Hum raha karte the pyar mein,
Aur humse jalta tha zamaana,

Kya bayaan karun faisal kya wo din the,
Khushi thi, hasi thi, rang tha, noor tha,
Jahan aaj ashq hai, dard hai, mayusi hai aur phaila hai veeraana!!!

-Faisal Khatri.
